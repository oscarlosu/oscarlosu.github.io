Artefacts

Role:
Designer, Programmer & Artist

Date:
Autumn 2017

For:
Exile Game Jam

Theme
"Not a Jam"

Software:
Unity
Blender

Artefacts is a collection of interactive toy puzzles. There are no instructions, each puzzle is a secret the player has to discover by exploring the different ways of interacting with each object.

When I started working on this project, I had a couple of interactions I knew I wanted to use and an idea of the player experience I wanted to create. In order to make this vision more concrete, I made three puzzles, each intended to make the player discover a new way of interacting with the objects in the game.
